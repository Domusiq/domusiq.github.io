Flat Fat Fartfest

For the game's sound design, I aimed to align each element with the theme and energy of each map while maintaining a consistent melodic and harmonic structure for a seamless musical experience. The audio evolves dynamically, adapting to different game stages. In the first level, set in an Asian world, I used traditional Chinese instruments and harmonies. The second level, a gym, features the same melody but with sounds that create a fresh, energetic atmosphere. The fast-food level incorporates commercial-inspired sounds, while the ‘sugar rush’ stage has fast-moving notes reflecting its chaotic excitement. The final level builds tension in a forest setting, blending nature and anticipation for the game’s climax.

Spockey

"Spockey" is a game where you control an astronaut through a space journey, uncovering mysteries while preventing a catastrophe by keeping antimatter instability in check. This game was my team's entry for the GMTK Game Jam 2024, and I was responsible for composing and recording the music and sound effects. The soundtrack was designed to enhance the player's experience across three distinct scenarios: feeling inspired and secure while exploring, building suspense as the story unfolds, and finally, evoking fear and urgency while escaping from the boss.

About Me.

I am a portuguese passionate musician and composer with a deep feeling for music and sound. My journey began at an early age in the violin. During my journey I explored various musical instruments, and nowadays I’m the drummer in bands… And I collect drumsticks all around the world.

Later on, I start composition and developing skills across various aspects of audio production. Over time, this passion has led me to work as a composer, editor, and sound designer, blending technical expertise with artistic creativity and strong intuiton.

I am currently working in the game audio industry, where I create immersive soundscapes, design sound effects, and compose original music to enhance interactive experiences. Whether it's composing, editing, or designing the sound, I bring a keen ear for detail and a strong understanding of how sound shapes emotion.

With experience in the industry since 2024, I have contributed to a variety of projects. Check them out!
